Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At LaRegularizacion.com, we respect your privacy. We collect:

  • Usage information: Pages visited, time on site, referrals

  • Cookies: For analytics and personalization (see Cookies section)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Show relevant ads (Google AdSense)

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for:
