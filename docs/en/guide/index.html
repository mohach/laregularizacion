Requirements & Documents

Hover over each item for practical tips.

Base requirements — all mandatory
1
In Spain before 01/01/2026
Any nominative document with a prior date: bills, medical records, bank statements, NGO records...
💡 Municipal registration is NOT required. Any document with your name and a date works.
2
5 months uninterrupted stay
No departures from Spain during the 5 months before application.
⚠️ Zero departures, even brief ones. Any trip may invalidate this requirement.
3
Over 18 years old
Adults only. Minors have separate procedures.
4
No serious criminal record
In Spain and countries of residence in the last 5 years.
💡 If your country doesn't respond within 1 month, submit proof of having requested the certificate.
5
Passport or travel document
Valid or expired. Registration certificate or recognized travel document also accepted.
💡 An expired passport is completely valid. No need to renew it before applying.
6
No other application in process
Except arraigos submitted before the decree came into force.
7
Tax form 790, code 052 — €38.28
Payment at an authorized bank. Keep the stamped receipt.
Plus: at least 1 of these 3 criteria
A
💼 Work ties
90 days worked in a year, job offer, or self-employment declaration.
B
👨‍👩‍👧 Family unit
Minor or disabled children, or first-degree relatives cohabiting in Spain.
C
🏘️ Social vulnerability
Certificate from social services or registered NGO.
💡 Asylum applicants (before 01/01/2026) are exempt from this additional criterion.
Asylum / International Protection applicants

If you applied for asylum before 01/01/2026, you can apply for regularization without withdrawing your asylum claim. Use form EX-31.

Who CANNOT apply
  • ❌People with valid legal residence or stay
  • ❌Those with another application in process
  • ❌People with a firm expulsion order

Frequently asked questions

FAQ

When can I apply?
Online: from April 16, 2026, available 24/7. In-person with prior appointment: from April 20. Deadline: June 30, 2026.
Which form: EX-31 or EX-32?
EX-31 if you applied for asylum before 01/01/2026. EX-32 if irregular situation and arrived before 01/01/2026 without applying for asylum. When in doubt call 060.
Can I use an expired passport?
Yes, explicitly. RD 316/2026 accepts valid or expired passports.
Can I work while waiting for the decision?
Yes. The work authorization is valid from the moment you register your application.
What documents prove my 5-month stay?
Any nominative document with a date: utility bills, medical records, bank statements, transport passes, rental contracts, payslips, NGO records...
Is municipal registration required?
No. It can be used as one of several proof documents, but is not mandatory.
Does this cover family members?
Yes, you can apply jointly with a partner and first-degree relatives.
How long does the permit last?
1 year, renewable. Valid for work in any sector across Spain.
